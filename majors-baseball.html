Majors Baseball

You will be amazed at the caliber of baseball played at this level! The game remains at a 46â€™ pitching mound and 60â€™ base path but some of these players can now pitch in excess of 60 miles per hour. Base stealing is allowed (no lead offs). Umpires are provided for each game. Both boys and girls aged 11 and 12 are welcome at this level, with the occasional skilled 10 year-old.

The Majors Division has a tournament team which will play in the District 7 (city-wide) Championships during the second week of July. The winner of that moves on to Provincials. The winner of Provincials moves on to Canadians. The winner of Canadians moves on to the Little League World Series in Williamsport, PA, USA!

All players must have a glove, cleats (no metal), pelvic protection (jock/jill), grey baseball pants, and batting helmet. Batting gloves are optional. Mouth guards are recommended. Please make sure that your player's name is clearly marked on all items. Each player will receive a Beacon Hill Little League team jersey, long-sleeved shirt, socks and hat. The jersey must be returned at the end of the season.

Beacon Hill Little League is run entirely by volunteers. We are very grateful to those that volunteer to coach each season! If you would like to coach a team, please visit COACH REGISTRATION for more information.

Player assessments for Majors Baseball will be held in March. Teams are then drafted with an emphasis on achieving balanced teams within the BHLL Majors Division. The draft makes it difficult for us to honour friend requests at this level.
The Majors Division requires tremendous player and parental commitment to baseball. The regular season runs from early April to mid-June with tournament play often extending into the first half of summer. Players can expect to practice twice a week and play 1-3 games each week. Game times are variable as our teams will interlock with other Victoria teams. You may travel within Victoria a handful of times during the season for baseball games.
