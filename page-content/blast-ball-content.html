Ages 3-4

Blast Ball introduces young children (ages 3-4) to the basic fundamentals of baseball - hitting, throwing, catching, running and fielding.

Kids hit a spongy ball off a stationary tee and run to bases that honk when you step on them! No score is kept and the focus is on fun, sportsmanship and safety. Suitable for both aspiring baseball and softball players (co-ed).

Locations

All practices and games take place on the lower field at Hollywood Park.

Time Commitment

Beginning in 2019, we will offer two options for BlastBall where parents can choose which of these fun hour-long sessions better suit their family schedule. If preferred, keen young players can attend both sessions at no extra cost. Sessions will be offered Wednesdays 5 - 6pm and Sundays 10 - 11am.

Equipment & Apparel

Players need to bring their own glove and batting helmet.
Wear running shoes.
Bats are supplied by the team. Bring your own too if you have a favorite!
Players receive a Beacon Hill hat and t-shirt which become their uniform for the season.
