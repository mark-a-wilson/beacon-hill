Purdy's Chocolate Fundraiser

South Victoria Capitals Baseball is fundraising with Purdy’s Chocolatier, just in time for Christmas!

If you’d like to support us through purchasing some very sweet Christmas gifts for friends, family and yourself, please click the link below:

PURDY’S CHOCOLATIER SEASONAL FUNDRAISING

After you’ve signed in you can buy the amazing chocolates with no additional mark up or invite others to join and support our Fundraising Campaign (44070). Pick up for the chocolates can be arranged after 13 December. Delivery will also be available by donation to the South Capitals.

If you are interested in playing ball at Royal Athletic Park with the South Victoria Capitals (ages 13-16) and have yet to register, please send an email to southcapitalsbaseball@gmail.com

Thank you for your support!
