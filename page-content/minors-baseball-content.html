Minors Baseball

Ages 9-10

Time to play baseball! Kids pitch from a 46â€™ pitching mound during regulation baseball games (six innings). Players continue to develop their baseball skills with a focus on good sportsmanship. Base stealing is allowed (no lead offs). Umpires are provided for each game. Both boys and girls aged 9 & 10 are welcome at this age, with the occasional skilled 7 or 8 year-old.

The Minors Division has a tournament team which will play in the District 7 (city-wide) Championships during the first week of July. The winner moves on to Provincials!

Locations

Practices occur at Brooke Street park, Hollywood Park, and the batting cage. House league and home games are played at Hollywood and you may travel within Victoria a handful of times during the season for intelock games.

Time Commitment

Players can expect to practice twice a week and play 1-2 games each week.

Team Selection

Player assessments for Minors Baseball are held in March. Teams are then drafted with an emphasis on achieving balanced teams. The draft makes it difficult for us to accommodate friend requests at this level.

Equipment & Apparel

Players must bring their own glove and batting helmet and wear pelvic protection (jock/jill).
Shoes with plastic cleats are required.
Bats are supplied by the team. Players may use their own bat as long as it has the USA Baseball™ stamp, signifying it's allowed for Little League play. Batting gloves are optional.
Each player will receive a Beacon Hill Little League team jersey, long-sleeved shirt, baseball pants, socks and hat. The jersey and pants must be returned at the end of the season.
