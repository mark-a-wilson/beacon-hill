Mini Minors

Ages 7-8

In Mini Minors, children receive additional instruction on the fundamentals of baseball â€“ hitting, throwing, catching, running and fielding â€“ with emphasis on playing positions in the field and good sportsmanship during game play. Players start the season by hitting from coach pitch, but after the May long weekend they will have the opportunity to practice hitting from player/coach pitch in addition to practicing pitching themselves if desired. Mini Minor games consist of 4 innings (maximum 2 hours long) with each player getting a turn at bat and all players playing the field in every inning. During a game, each team will have 3 outs or a maximum of 3 runs per at bat per inning. Suitable for both aspiring baseball and softball players (co-ed). If appropriate, 8 year old players who demonstrate the required level of skill and maturity may be assessed for Minors Baseball (co-ed) or Minors Softball (girls).

All players must have a glove and batting helmet. Please make sure that your player's name is clearly marked on all items. Each player will receive a Beacon Hill Little League team t-shirt and hat at the start of the season. The team t-shirt becomes your team uniform for the season.
Beacon Hill Little League is run entirely by volunteers. We are very grateful to those that volunteer to coach each season! If you would like to coach a team, please visit COACH REGISTRATION for more information. Teams will be selected to ensure a mix of ages and genders. When dividing our youngest players into teams at the Mini Minors level, we make every effort to honour friend and coach requests to maintain a high level of enthusiasm for the game.
Mini Minors players play on the lower field at Hollywood Park. Each team will generally practice once a week and have one game per week, usually on a Saturday or Sunday.
