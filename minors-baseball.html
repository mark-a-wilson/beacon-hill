Ages 9-10
Minors Baseball

Time to play baseball! Kids pitch from a 46â€™ pitching mound during regulation baseball games (six innings). Players continue to develop their baseball skills with a focus on good sportsmanship. Base stealing is allowed (no lead offs). Umpires are provided for each game. Both boys and girls aged 9 & 10 are welcome at this age, with the occasional skilled 7 or 8 year-old.

The Minors Division has a tournament team which will play in the District 7 (city-wide) Championships during the first week of July. The winner moves on to Provincials!

All players must have a glove, cleats (no metal), pelvic protection (jock/jill), and batting helmet. Batting gloves are optional. Mouth guards are recommended. Please make sure that your player's name is clearly marked on all items. Each player will receive a Beacon Hill Little League team jersey, long-sleeved shirt, baseball pants, socks and hat. The jersey and pants must be returned at the end of the season.

Beacon Hill Little League is run entirely by volunteers. We are very grateful to those that volunteer to coach each season! If you would like to coach a team, please visit COACH REGISTRATION for more information.

Player assessments for Minors Baseball will be held in March. Teams are then drafted with an emphasis on achieving balanced teams within the BHLL Minors Division. The draft makes it difficult for us to honour friend requests at this level.
Players can expect to practice twice a week and play 1-2 games each week. Game times are variable as our teams will interlock with other Victoria teams. You may travel within Victoria a handful of times during the season for baseball games.
