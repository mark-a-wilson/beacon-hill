Blast Ball
Blast Ball introduces young children (ages 3-4) to the basic fundamentals of baseball - hitting, throwing, catching, running and fielding. Kids hit a spongy ball off a stationary tee and run to bases that honk when you step on them. No score is kept and the focus is on fun, sportsmanship and safety. Suitable for both aspiring baseball and softball players (co-ed).
All players must have a glove and batting helmet. Please make sure that your player's name is clearly marked on all items. Each player will receive a Beacon Hill Little League t-shirt and hat at the start of the season.
Beacon Hill Little League is run entirely by volunteers. We are very grateful to those that volunteer to coach each season! If you would like to coach a team, please visit COACH REGISTRATION for more information. Although we do not divide players into specific teams at the Blast Ball level, we always encourage enthusiastic parent coaching support. We are also lucky to have many of our club's older baseball and softball players helping out with Blast Ball to help inspire the next generation of ball players!
Blast Ball players play on the lower field at Hollywood Park. Beginning in 2019, we will offer two options for BlastBall where parents can choose which of these fun hour-long sessions better suit their family schedule. If preferred, keen young players can attend both sessions at no extra cost. Sessions will be offered Wednesdays 5 - 6pm and Sundays 10 - 11am.
