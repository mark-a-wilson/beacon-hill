In Mini Minors, children receive additional instruction on the fundamentals of baseball â€“ hitting, throwing, catching, running and fielding â€“ with emphasis on playing positions in the field and good sportsmanship during game play.

Players start the season by hitting from coach pitch, but after the May long weekend they will have the opportunity to practice hitting from player/coach pitch in addition to practicing pitching themselves if desired. Mini Minor games consist of 4 innings (maximum 2 hours long) with each player getting a turn at bat and all players playing the field in every inning. During a game, each team will have 3 outs or a maximum of 3 runs per at bat per inning. Suitable for both aspiring baseball and softball players (co-ed). If appropriate, 8 year old players who demonstrate the required level of skill and maturity may be assessed for Minors Baseball (co-ed) or Minors Softball (girls).

Locations

All practices and games take place on the lower field at Hollywood Park.

Time Commitment

Teams generally have one practice a week on a weeknight and one game on Saturday or Sunday.

Teams are selected to ensure a mix of ages and genders. In Mini Minors, we make every effort to accommodate friend and coach requests to maintain a high level of enthusiasm for the game.

Equipment & Apparel

Players need to bring their own glove and batting helmet.
Wear running shoes.
Bats are supplied by the team. Players may use their own bat as long as it has the USA Baseball™ stamp, signifying it's allowed for Little League play. Batting gloves are optional.
Players receive a Beacon Hill hat and t-shirt which become their uniform for the season.
