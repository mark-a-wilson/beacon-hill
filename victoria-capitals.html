Victoria Capitals

Want to keep playing baseball with Beacon Hill Little League after age 12?

FUN competitive baseball is now available for players up to age 16!

The Victoria Capitals is an extension of the Little League program where players from Beacon Hill team up with players from other local Little League clubs to form the South Victoria Capitals. This program was created so players can remain in their local neighbourhood parks playing fun, competitive baseball. New this year, all home games for the South Victoria Capitals will be played at ROYAL ATHLETIC PARK! The Capitals will play against local teams, teams from up-island, teams from the mainland, and possibly teams from Seattle and Port Angeles.

  • Experienced coaching
  • FREE training by ex-college players (up to 7.5 hrs)
  • Spring Registration fee of $150 to play 11 weeks
  • Practice twice a week
  • Play 1-2 games per week
  • Local tournaments
